What is mypokemon.io map?

This Pokemon map shows you where are the pokemons and the estimated time they are going to disappear.

You can find all Pokemon near you in real time for Pokemon Go App Game. Pokemon will be marked on the map along with their disappearence count down timer on the map. The map marker is real time, meaning they are currently exists in the Pokemon Go app and can be found exact same spot on the map.

Why there is no pokemon in my area?

Since we are waiting for our agents to update pokemon location from various devices, it might take a while for them to check different location and update the map. If you do not see pokemon for over 24 hours, please contact support@mypokemon.io

Why do I see some Pokemon in game but not on your map?

Sorry, our map data is not perfect at this time, some pokemons might missing from the map.

Zoom in the map and wait for one more minute to see if it helps.

Any tips?

Open the map and zoom in to the area you can reach, then keep your phone open so that map will update automatically

When you see the pokemon you want to catch, go catch it!

Note: Rarer pokemons might disappear sooner than normal pokemons. Make sure to keep an eye on the timers!
